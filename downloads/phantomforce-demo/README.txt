PHANTOMFORCE DEMO KIT
=====================

Thanks for requesting the PhantomForce demo.

Open `phantomforce-demo.html` in your browser. It is a local, self-contained
preview of the business-owner workflow:

1. Capture a lead or customer message.
2. Classify the business risk and priority.
3. Draft a safe response that stays under owner approval.
4. Queue the next follow-up.

The full PhantomForce app lives at:

https://app.phantomforce.online

Paid subscriptions receive a private PF-PRO access key by email. Keep that key
private; it is used to unlock paid PhantomForce access.
