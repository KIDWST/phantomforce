PHANTOMFORCE SETUP / DEMO KIT
=============================

Thanks for requesting the PhantomForce setup/demo.

Open `phantomforce-demo.html` in your browser. It is a local, self-contained
preview of the business-owner workflow PhantomForce can help set up:

1. Capture a lead or customer message.
2. Classify the business risk and priority.
3. Draft a safe response that stays under owner approval.
4. Queue the next follow-up.

PhantomForce is AI-assisted operations plus real-world execution:

* websites and stores
* lead capture and follow-up
* booking and approval workflows
* client dashboards and status tracking
* ChicagoShots media/content support
* controlled Media Lab creative generation

Client work is scoped and approved before any live system, message, media asset,
or automation goes out.
